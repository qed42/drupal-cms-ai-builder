# TASK-340: SaaS Onboarding UX Research

**Story:** US-063
**Priority:** P0
**Estimated Effort:** L
**Milestone:** M18 — UX Revamp

## Description
Analyze onboarding flows of top SaaS products to identify patterns applicable to an AI website builder. Document progressive disclosure, live previews, contextual guidance, trust signals, and split-pane layouts that can inform the redesign of our onboarding experience.

## Technical Approach
1. Audit onboarding flows of: Notion, Linear, Vercel, Framer, Webflow, Squarespace, Wix, Canva, Figma, Stripe
2. For each, document:
   - Number of steps and information density per step
   - Use of empty space (split-pane, side panels, contextual cards)
   - Progressive disclosure patterns
   - Trust signals and social proof placement
   - How they handle AI/automation transparency (if applicable)
   - Mobile vs. desktop layout differences
3. Synthesize patterns into categories: "Applicable to our builder" vs. "Not relevant"
4. Create wireframe sketches for 3 layout options based on findings
5. Deliver as a research document in `project-management/sprint-outputs/`

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Research document covers at least 8 of the 10 target products
- [ ] Document identifies top 5 patterns applicable to our AI builder context
- [ ] At least 3 wireframe layout options with pros/cons
- [ ] Recommendation on which layout direction to pursue
