# US-015: CTA Generation & Placement

**Epic:** AI Website Generation
**Priority:** P0
**Milestone:** M2 — AI Site Generation Engine

## User Story
As a business owner, I want the AI to generate and place relevant calls-to-action throughout my website, so that visitors are guided toward converting (booking, contacting, requesting a quote).

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Given the user provided CTA phrases during onboarding, when pages are generated, then those CTAs are placed on relevant pages
- [ ] Given no explicit CTAs were provided, when pages are generated, then the AI infers appropriate CTAs from the business context
- [ ] Given a Services page, when generated, then each service section includes a contextual CTA
- [ ] Given the Home page, when generated, then it includes at least one prominent above-the-fold CTA
- [ ] Given CTAs are placed, when clicked, then they navigate to the Contact page or relevant conversion page

## Dependencies
- US-013 (Page Generation)
- US-008 (Business Context — CTA phrases)

## Technical Notes
- FR-204
- CTAs rendered as SDC button/CTA components from Space theme
- CTA destinations must be internal links to generated pages (not broken links)
