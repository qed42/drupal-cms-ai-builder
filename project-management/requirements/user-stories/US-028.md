# US-028: Smart Form Fields

**Epic:** Lead Capture & Forms
**Priority:** P2
**Milestone:** M4 — Lead Capture & Forms

## User Story
As a business owner, I want the AI to suggest contextually appropriate form fields based on my industry and page, so that my forms capture the right information from leads.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Given a Healthcare contact form, when generated, then AI suggests fields like "Insurance Provider", "Preferred appointment date"
- [ ] Given a Real Estate contact form, when generated, then AI suggests fields like "Property interest", "Budget range", "Pre-approved (yes/no)"
- [ ] Given suggested fields, when displayed during editing, then the user can accept, remove, or modify suggested fields
- [ ] Given a non-contact page with a CTA, when a form is needed, then AI suggests a shorter form appropriate to the context (e.g., "Get a Quote" form with fewer fields)

## Dependencies
- US-026 (Contact Form Auto-Generation)
- US-009 (Dynamic Industry Questions — for industry context)

## Technical Notes
- FR-402
- AI suggests fields during generation; user edits via Canvas or Webform UI
- Field suggestions are industry-specific prompt templates
