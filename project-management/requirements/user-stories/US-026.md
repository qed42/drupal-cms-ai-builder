# US-026: Contact Form Auto-Generation

**Epic:** Lead Capture & Forms
**Priority:** P0
**Milestone:** M4 — Lead Capture & Forms

## User Story
As a business owner, I want the AI to automatically generate a contact form on my Contact page, so that visitors can reach out to me without me having to build a form manually.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Given the Contact page is generated, when viewed, then it includes a contact form with fields: Name, Email, Phone (optional), Message
- [ ] Given an industry with specific needs (e.g., Healthcare), when the form is generated, then it includes relevant additional fields (e.g., "Preferred appointment time")
- [ ] Given the contact form, when a visitor submits it, then the data is validated (required fields, email format)
- [ ] Given a valid form submission, when submitted, then a success message is displayed to the visitor

## Dependencies
- US-013 (Page Generation — Contact page must exist)

## Technical Notes
- FR-401
- Built with Drupal Webform module or custom form entities
- Form fields determined by AI based on industry and business context
- Form rendered as an SDC component from the Space theme
