# US-010: Compliance Inference During Onboarding

**Epic:** Onboarding Wizard
**Priority:** P1
**Milestone:** M1 — Platform Foundation & Onboarding

## User Story
As a new user in a regulated industry, I want the AI to identify relevant compliance requirements and ask me about them during onboarding, so that my website includes appropriate disclaimers and compliant elements.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Given a Healthcare industry selection, when the AI generates questions, then it includes compliance-related questions (e.g., "Do you need HIPAA-compliant forms?")
- [ ] Given a Legal industry selection, when the AI generates questions, then it asks about attorney advertising rules and jurisdiction-specific disclaimers
- [ ] Given compliance requirements are identified, when the site is generated, then appropriate disclaimers are included on relevant pages
- [ ] Given a non-regulated industry (e.g., Restaurant), when the AI evaluates compliance, then it focuses on general requirements (privacy policy, terms of service, accessibility)

## Dependencies
- US-009 (Dynamic Industry Questions)

## Technical Notes
- FR-106
- Compliance inference is part of the Industry Agent's logic — not a separate step, but embedded in the dynamic questions
- Compliance flags stored on the site profile entity and passed to the Content Agent during generation
- AI must include a disclaimer that compliance content is AI-generated and should be reviewed by legal counsel
