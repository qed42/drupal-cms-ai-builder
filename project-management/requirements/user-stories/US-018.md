# US-018: Industry-Specific Content & Disclaimers

**Epic:** AI Website Generation
**Priority:** P1
**Milestone:** M2 — AI Site Generation Engine

## User Story
As a business owner in a regulated industry, I want the AI to generate content that includes appropriate industry terminology and compliance disclaimers, so that my website is professionally relevant and appropriately cautious.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Given a Healthcare site, when content is generated, then medical terminology is used appropriately and a general medical disclaimer is included
- [ ] Given a Legal site, when content is generated, then legal terminology is used and an "Attorney Advertising" disclaimer is included where required
- [ ] Given any compliance flags from onboarding (US-010), when content is generated, then relevant disclaimers are placed on appropriate pages (footer, contact page)
- [ ] Given any generated disclaimer, when viewed, then it includes a notice: "This disclaimer was AI-generated. Please review with your legal counsel."
- [ ] Given a non-regulated industry, when content is generated, then standard privacy policy and terms placeholders are included

## Dependencies
- US-010 (Compliance Inference)
- US-014 (SEO Content Generation)

## Technical Notes
- FR-209
- Content Agent uses industry-specific prompt templates
- Disclaimers stored as reusable Drupal blocks or paragraph components
- Disclaimer placement rules: footer for general, inline for page-specific
